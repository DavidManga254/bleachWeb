BLEACH

ブリーチ Bleach

Bleach is a manga by Taito Kubo and its adaptation. The manga was published from August 2001 to August 2016 in Shonen Jump magazine. Currently, the manga is finished and has 686 chapters combined into 74 volumes. In 2005, the manga «Bleach» was awarded the Shogakukan publishing house award as the best manga in the senen category (for boys), it is also included in the list of best-selling comics in Japan and the USA.
